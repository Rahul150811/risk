Welcome to Our Cybersecurity Risk Assessment Tool

Assess your cybersecurity practices and improve your online security.

Risk Assessment Questions

Password Security:

a. Do you use strong and unique passwords for your online accounts?

b. How often do you change your passwords?

c. Do you use a password manager to store and generate complex passwords?

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):

a. Do you enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible?

b. Are you aware of the types of MFA available (e.g., SMS codes, authenticator apps, hardware tokens)?

Device Security:

a. Do you keep your computer, smartphone, and other devices up to date with the latest security patches and updates?

b. Do you use security software (e.g., antivirus) on your devices?

c. Is your smartphone protected with a PIN, password, or biometric authentication?

Social Engineering Awareness:

a. Are you cautious about sharing personal information online or over the phone?

b. Do you know how to recognize phishing emails and scam calls?

Data Backup:

a. Do you regularly back up your important data and files?

b. How often do you test your backups to ensure they can be restored?

Software and App Downloads:

a. Do you only download software and apps from reputable sources (e.g., official app stores)?

b. Are you cautious about granting permissions to apps on your devices?

Social Media Privacy:

a. Have you reviewed and adjusted your privacy settings on social media platforms?

b. Do you limit the amount of personal information you share publicly on social media?
